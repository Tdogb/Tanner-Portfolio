[Project in progress] This project's motivation is to do the impossible and create balancing onewheel using a mechanically connected gas engine. The engine is salvaged from a leaf blower and feeds into a differential along with an electric motor to produce positive and negative torque. I ported VESC Float Package to a Teensy which is running the control loop.
