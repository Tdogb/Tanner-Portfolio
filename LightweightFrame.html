RPM Limiter

Personal

Lightweight drone racing frame designed to be as stiff and as light as possible. The weight ends up being 235.8g, allowing the drone to have a ~10:1 power to weight ratio. The innovation in the frame is reduced screw count down to only 7 screws while still allowing for replacement of the arms. CNC cut in china with T700 carbon fiber
