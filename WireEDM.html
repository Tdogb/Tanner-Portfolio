DIY Wire EDM

Personal

Wire EDM I designed and built for a Hackathon. Runs off of a custom control board I designed. The control board runs off of a 12s lipo battery, and generates sparks at 1MHz. I was able to cut through about 1mm of aluminum, at which point the dielectric pump needed to be upgraded to get a dielectric flow through the thin cut area
