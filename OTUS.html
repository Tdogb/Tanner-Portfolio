OTUS

Personal

Collaborative system designed to deploy drone into tornado wind field and measure inflow winds ahead of the thunderstorm. As a species we still do not possess the ability to predict tornado genesis which poses a major safety hazard. The plane and drone will collect data on the wind speed, temperature, pressure and humidity and reports in real time to the ground station. All programming based off off Ardupilot and Betaflight open source software.

Skills
